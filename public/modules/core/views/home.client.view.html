You found your way!

I’m Laurie, a facilitator, planner, thinker, noob developer, mom, dog lover and more. This is friendly road, a place where people work on something together while finding common ground along the way with others in unexpected ways. I’m on a mission to get everyone to put ideas to action by telling someone, learning, and paying it forward.

Inspiration

After attending events, getting new training, meeting people, I shared and heard many new ideas. Wanting to share the power of collaboration, I set out to create a way for others to work on ideas with meaning and purpose.

The goal is to be a place to collect, share, and do things and see what comes out of it.

Collaboration

I believe that before the brand, a social media plan, or a website, comes the journey. As such I have prepared some worksops to start with your something. Make it a journey. Play with your inspiration. Fight your roadblocks. Make twists and turns along the way. A website is just one part.

In my journey to learn code, I made this website.

Results

We’ve yet to see! A finished website, a new team, an idea for commericializing, a new product, a new passion. Each journey brings new outcomes. A new start, collaboration, from inspiration to tangable stuff.

I believe the creative journey creates true value.
